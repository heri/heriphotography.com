Slow-motion cameras : DSLR, mirrorless & cinema cameras doing 120fps and more

March 18, 2014 Video Gear, video production 1080p cinematography Panasonic slow motion video slow-motion sony 2 Comments

Slow motion video can really add significant production value to your film – wowing young & old minds alike. However cameras that are capable of cine-like are rare, information is spare, so that’s why we took the initiative to list them in a handy specs table.

Other cameras that you can look into are : Panasonic HC-V750 (120fps 1080p, small 1/2.3″ sensor), GoPro Black edition, Sony F55 CineAlta or RED Dragon (starts at $50k), Phantom Cameras (only for rentals). Also don’t forget that most cameras can do 60fps video which is a great start, before you think of getting a dedicated camera.

      I’ll second Peter’s comment, and clear up some of the mis-information you’ve provided on the Scarlet.
