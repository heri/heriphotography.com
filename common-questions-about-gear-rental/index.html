Common questions about gear rental

Below are some questions we often get from our customers about the process of renting equipment from us. If you can’t find an answer to your question here, please contact us with the form below and we’ll be glad to help.

Is payment required when reserving?

Yes – after the date agreement, we will send you an invoice that you can pay with Paypal or Credit Card.

Can I just walk in and hire some equipment without first making a booking?

No – all hires must be booked in advance. If you are a new customer, we will have to confirm the ID you need to bring with you on your first visit to open your account.

Is a security deposit required?

Not usually, but you need to be a Canadian citizen or a Canadian permanent resident to hire from us, and you’ll need proof of this (your passport or a statement from Citizen and Immigration Canada). Bring also a photo ID. You’ll also need proof of your current address (utility bill, bank statement etc issued in the last 3 months.) and a valid credit card, which will be validated and photocopied. The photocopy of your credit card will be held for the duration of your booking.

A deposit or bank guarantee may be requested in circumstances where the hire is considered unusually risky. There is no definitive definition of risky but a deposit could be requested where, for example, you’ve booked a range of expensive equipment for your first booking etc.

You will be advised as soon as possible after making your booking if a security deposit is required.

How long is a “day” in hire terms?

You can usually collect the equipment you have booked from 3pm the day before the first day of your booking and return it by 12pm the day following the last day of your booking. So, for example, if you book for a Tuesday and Wednesday, you can collect your booking from 3pm until closing time on Monday (or from opening time on Tuesday) and return it by 12pm on Thursday

We will call you if there is any delay that might prevent you collecting your booking from 3pm the day before your booking starts.

Where can I find the hire agreement?

If you’re looking for the legal terms and conditions applicable to hiring our camera equipment, you’ll always find the latest version at our hire agreement page.

What happens if I want to cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking without charge up to 48 hours prior to the first day of your booking. Cancellations later than 48 hours prior to the first day of your booking incur a fee of 50% of the booking.

If you make a booking and don’t show up by the end of the first day of the booking, you’ll be considered a no-show and this incurs a fee of the full cost of the booking.

Please cancel as early as possible so that other customers may re-book the equipment you no longer want.

Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on site and credit cards and Paypal online.
